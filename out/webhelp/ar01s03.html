Staff and Barlines

When you hit the spacebar you draw a new staff “block”. Before any note or chord, you have to draw a new staff block where this note or chord will sit.

The barlines are located as follows:

Musical symbolComputer key
simple barline,
double barline,,
final barline.
repeat barline (start);
repeat barline (end):

Cavatina is not made to do minor adjustments to the layout, but if you consider it necessary, you can gain control over horizontal spacing by using the half space - and the quarter space --.
